Three swappable app designs

Pick a concept to preview. Each one is a complete, brand-themed look for the entire ECS FC app — same data and features, three different design languages.

Switching changes the entire app UI/UX (navigation, chrome, and page content) for the logged-in user. An admin flips between all three to A/B test them on the same real pages.
