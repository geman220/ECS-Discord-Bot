Delete Your Account

ECS FC Mobile App — Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: March 28, 2026

This action is permanent. Once your account is deleted, your personal data is erased and cannot be recovered. Please read this page carefully before proceeding.

How to Delete Your Account

From the Mobile App

  1. Open the ECS FC app and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings (tap the gear icon).
  3. Scroll to Account and tap Delete Account.
  4. Enter your password and type DELETE MY ACCOUNT to confirm.
  5. Your account and personal data will be permanently deleted.

From the Web

You can also manage your account from the web portal:

Go to Account Settings

Can't Access Your Account?

If you are unable to sign in, you can request account deletion by emailing donotreply@weareecs.com from the email address associated with your account. Include your username and we will process your request within 7 business days.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following happens immediately:

Data Type Action
Email address Permanently erased
Password Permanently erased
Username Replaced with anonymous identifier
Two-factor authentication secret Permanently erased
Push notification tokens Deactivated immediately
Active sessions Revoked immediately (logged out everywhere)
Notification preferences Reset / no longer applicable

What Data Is Retained

To maintain league integrity (match results, standings, historical records), certain non-identifying data is retained in anonymized form:

Data Type Retention
Match results and scores Retained (anonymized)
Season and career statistics Retained (anonymized)
RSVP / attendance history Retained (anonymized)
Retained records are associated with an anonymous identifier (e.g. deleted_user_123) and cannot be traced back to you. No personal information remains in these records.

Data Export

Before deleting your account, you can download a copy of all your personal data. In the app, go to Settings → Download My Data. You will receive an email with a download link (JSON format) within a few minutes.

Questions?

If you have questions about your data or this process, please contact us at donotreply@weareecs.com.
