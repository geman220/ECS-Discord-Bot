Privacy Policy for ECS Soccer League ChatGPT Assistant

Effective Date: June 4, 2025
Last Updated: June 4, 2025

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how the ECS Soccer League ChatGPT Assistant ("the Assistant") collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with our custom GPT integration.

Information We Access

From Our ECS Database

The Assistant has read-only access to:

  • Player Information: Names, positions, team affiliations, statistics, and public profile data
  • Team Data: Team names, rosters, standings, and match information
  • Match Records: Scores, dates, locations, and game events
  • League Information: Standings, schedules, and seasonal data

From Your Interactions

When you use the Assistant, we may collect:

  • Query Content: The questions and requests you ask
  • Usage Patterns: How you interact with the Assistant
  • Session Information: Technical data about your interactions

How We Use Information

We use the information to:

  • Provide Responses: Answer your questions about league data
  • Improve Service: Enhance the Assistant's performance and accuracy
  • Maintain Security: Monitor for misuse and ensure system integrity

Information We Share

We DO NOT Share:

  • Personal Contact Information: Phone numbers, email addresses, or private details
  • Sensitive Data: Internal notes, private communications, or confidential information
  • User Queries: Your specific questions or interactions with third parties

We DO Share:

  • Public League Data: Information already available through our public channels
  • Aggregated Statistics: Non-identifying usage patterns for improvement purposes

Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • API Key Authentication: Secure access controls
  • Read-Only Access: The Assistant cannot modify or delete data
  • Encrypted Communications: All data transmission is encrypted
  • Limited Scope: Access restricted to publicly available league information

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about what data is accessible
  • Correction: Request updates to inaccurate public profile information
  • Deletion: Request removal of your data from our systems
  • Restriction: Limit how your information is processed

OpenAI Integration

This Assistant operates through OpenAI's ChatGPT platform. Please review:

  • OpenAI's Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/privacy/
  • OpenAI's Terms of Service: https://openai.com/terms/

Your interactions with the Assistant are subject to both this policy and OpenAI's policies.

Data Retention

  • Query Data: Processed in real-time, not permanently stored by us
  • League Data: Maintained according to our standard retention policies
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated data may be retained for service improvement

Children's Privacy

This service is not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Continued use of the Assistant constitutes acceptance of any changes.

Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or requests:

Website: https://portal.ecsfc.com

Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • OpenAI's Custom GPT requirements
Note: This Assistant provides information about our soccer league based on publicly available data. For official league business or sensitive matters, please contact us directly through our official channels.

This policy was last reviewed on June 4, 2025 and applies specifically to the ECS Soccer League ChatGPT Assistant integration.
