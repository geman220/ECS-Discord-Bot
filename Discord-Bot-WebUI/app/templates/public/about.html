ECS Pub League is where Seattle comes to play. We're a radically inclusive, beginner-friendly adult soccer league built on one simple idea: soccer for all.

Whether you're a lifelong player or you've never touched a ball, there's a place for you here. We care more about who you are than how you play, and our community extends far beyond the pitch.

What makes us different

  • Everyone plays. No benchwarming, no gatekeeping.
  • Two divisions — Classic and Premier — so you can find your pace.
  • A real community that hangs out on and off the field.

New players start by coming to a Pub League Open Practice (PLOP) and getting approved — then you're in.
