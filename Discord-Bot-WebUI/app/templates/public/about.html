Beginner soccer in Seattle

We play 8v8 (including goalkeepers) on a half-size turf field, with unlimited substitutions and shifts of 5–10 minutes. Two ten-week seasons a year — Spring (April–June) and Fall (Labor Day–Thanksgiving) — scheduled around Sounders matches.

We form new teams every season, so this year's opponents become next year's teammates. Each team picks its own name, theme, and custom jersey — and there's an end-of-season party at a sponsor pub.

{# ---- Divisions ---- #}

Two divisions, everybody plays

Classic

For players with little to no experience, or anyone easing back into the game. A slower pace, a teaching environment, and equal playing time for everyone. Haven't played in years? Never played at all? Come on out anyway.

Premier

A slightly higher level of friendly competition — still low/no contact and laid-back, with the same focus on development, team play, and fun. If you've played GSSL, RATS, or Arena, this probably isn't the league for you.

{# ---- Everybody plays + cups ---- #}

Everybody plays

We hold "tryouts" only to build evenly balanced teams — not to cut anyone. Everyone gets equal playing time regardless of ability or fitness, and every team makes the playoffs for a shot at the Pub League Cup.

Unlimited subs and short shifts make it a great place to learn the game or get back into it later in life. Week 9 is Fun Week — costumes, a potluck BBQ, and the ever-popular "multiball."

{# ---- PLOP ---- #}

Start with a PLOP

Most Sundays in the summer and winter offseasons we hold free, open Pub League Offseason Practices. PLOP is the best way to check us out — and it's how we find the right place for you to play. All new players attend a PLOP and get approved before registering.

Our mission

Fun and friendly soccer for adults. We're radically inclusive — but we're not for everyone.

We're soccer fans of beginner to low-intermediate levels who get together to play in a fun, safe, organized environment for the love of the game and a little friendly competition.
