    These send without anyone pressing a button. A rule watches for something to happen — a league finishing its draft, the season turning over — waits the configured delay, then emails whoever matches its audience.

    Every send is recorded as a normal email blast, so you can open it and see exactly who received it and whether it landed. A rule fires at most once per league per season.
