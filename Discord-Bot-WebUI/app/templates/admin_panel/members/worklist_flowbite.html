    How Members works

    {%- set base = 'rounded-lg p-2.5 -m-0.5' -%} {%- macro hl(t) -%}{% if tab == t %} bg-ecs-green/5 ring-1 ring-ecs-green/30{% endif %}{%- endmacro -%}
    One person, one Hub. Every row opens that person’s Member Hub — the single place to approve/deny, set roles, place on a team, manage sub status, and handle the waitlist. Everything is inline; nothing sends you to a different page.
    All Members. Search or filter to reach anyone — approved or not, in a queue or not — including people you just want to approve or edit directly. The green Playing badge = on the roster this season.
    Approval ≠ Waitlist. They’re independent. Someone can be approved and waitlisted, or unapproved and waitlisted. The waitlist is a point-in-time, per-season list that clears at each season boundary.
    Subs play free. A sub never pays and isn’t a season “active” member — they help where/when needed and can also sit on the waitlist for a permanent spot.
    Quick Profiles. Field-created walk-ins. Convert them to a real account, link them to an existing player, message them, or delete — actions adapt to each profile’s status so you only see what’s valid.
    Legacy pages. The old approvals / waitlist / all-users / quick-profile pages still exist in the sidebar as a fallback during burn-in — this surface stands on its own and doesn’t depend on them.
