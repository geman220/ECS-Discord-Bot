    Compose a Message New

    Write once and deliver by app alert, push, email, Discord DM, or SMS — in one go. Each member only receives channels they have turned on, and you can schedule it for later.

    Send an Email Blast

    A one-time email to a division, team, role, or members you pick. Use saved layouts so every email looks consistent.

    Send a Push Notification

    An alert to phones with the ECS app installed. Sends immediately — the Scheduled & History tab handles future sends.

    Message One Person

    A direct SMS or Discord DM to a single player — for individual follow-ups.

    Post a Homepage Announcement

    A banner at the top of the member homepage. It does not notify anyone — pair it with an email or push to reach people directly.
