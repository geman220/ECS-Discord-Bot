    How engagement is measured

    Coach Score (0–100) blends three things: duties (40% — reports, lineups, reminders), availability (35% — the coach's OWN RSVP: do they respond and say yes to their team's matches), and chat (25% — presence in their team channel). Hover a score to see the breakdown. Green ≥70, amber 40–69, red <40.

    Own RSVP is the coach's personal availability (yes / total matches) — a coach who never RSVPs or never says yes scores low here regardless of paperwork.

    Activity score / Contribution % measures workload share within the team (who's pulling weight vs. coach-in-name-only); the Coach Score measures overall how a coach is doing.

    Last seen is the most recent of their webui login, mobile-app session, or any pub-league Discord message (hover for the breakdown); it turns amber past ~10 days. "Game-day only" flags a coach who posts in their team channel only on weekends with no midweek presence — fine for a player, a yellow flag for a coach.

    Reports & lineups are attributed across both web and the Flutter mobile app. Note "X/Y reported" counts matches that have a score (from any source) — it won't equal the per-coach Reports/Verified columns, since a match can be reported by the opponent or entered score-only.

    Goals logged compares goal-scorer events to the team's actual scores in reported matches. A score of 2 implies 2 goals should have scorers; if they're missing, the score was entered but the scorers weren't. Own goals count, and a small tolerance is allowed — a flagged gap (≥3 missing) means a team is routinely skipping the detail, not an edge case.

    RSVP checks are counted when a coach opens RSVPs on web or mobile. Checking only via the Discord embed can't be detected, so a low RSVP count isn't proof a coach never looked.

    Team chat counts a coach's messages in their own team's Discord channel — the main signal for whether they're present for their team. The small "all-PL" figure beneath it is their total across all pub-league channels, so you can spot a coach who's chatty in general chat but silent in their own team's channel. (Only pub-league channels are tracked, and this + RSVP-view tracking only accrue from when tracking was enabled.)
