    How the waitlist works show hide

    The waitlist is a reserve lane. People here have registered but are not approved to play in a league — they wait until a spot opens (e.g. an injury) so you can pull one in.

    • Approve & Assign League — takes them OFF the waitlist and INTO a league (as a member) or the sub pool (as a substitute). This is how you pull someone in.
    • The same dropdown's Waitlist options just move them to a different lane (e.g. Classic → Premier waitlist) without approving them.
    • Remove from Waitlist — takes them off the reserve entirely and returns them to the pending approvals queue for a fresh decision.
    • Being on the waitlist doesn't stop you inviting them to a practice — it only means they aren't rostered or approved yet.
