    Put one of these on a screen at the party, share it into Discord, or print it as a poster. Scanning opens the ECS FC app if the player has it, or their browser if they don't — either way it lands on the WooCommerce product page, where the presale password and the payment are handled. We never see a card.

    These codes never expire. They carry no season and no product — the product is resolved when the code is scanned, from whichever Pub League season is current. Print once, reuse every season.
