    Print these for the pre-season party. Scanning opens the ECS FC app if the player has it, or their browser if they don't — either way it lands on the WooCommerce product page, where the presale password and the payment are handled. We never see a card.

    These codes never expire. They carry no season and no product — the product is resolved when the code is scanned, from whichever Pub League season is current. Print once, reuse every season.

    {# --- iOS deep-link rollout switch --- #}

    Open season-pass links in the iOS app

    When off, the QR and pass links open in the phone browser for everyone — the reliable baseline that works on any phone. When on, iPhones with the app open the native flow instead.

    Turn this on only after the season-pass app version is live in the App Store. iOS routes these links to whatever app version a person has installed — flip it too early and someone on an older app hits a dead end. Android is unaffected (it's gated by the app itself), and the browser flow always works regardless.

    {% if deeplinks_enabled %}ON — iPhones with the app open the native flow.{% else %}OFF — everyone uses the browser flow.{% endif %}
